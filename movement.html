The Flux movement is an international effort to promote a better system of democracy across the globe, one which will deliver better policy, include more voices, and have more prosperous outcomes for our society.

The movement is one of political expression, not ideology. Its principle endeavour is the success of non-partisan political parties, which provide citizens direct access to real political power via The Flux System outside of election cycles.  

The Flux Foundation serves as a link between Flux chapters and provides direction, cohesion, support and technology to the movement.
